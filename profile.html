Chef Claire Smith Bio

Chef Claireâ€™s flavors stem from being raised by her grandmother along the Appalachian trail in Caratunk, Maine near the base of Moxie mountain. She moved to NYC to pursue her dreams of becoming a french chef and studied French Cuisine. After graduating she moved to to Paris to work as a sous chef. Later she was recruited by Michelin Star chef Jean-Georges to be the Chef De Cuisine . After 4 years of mastering his style and creating one of the most sold out menu items; she wanted to create her own place. Her vision was a homey type of place where her mountain roots of comfort food could be enjoyed in suburban NYC.
